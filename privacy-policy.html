Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

VoxelInk Studios operates a social media content scheduling platform that allows users to connect their TikTok accounts and publish video content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our platform and third-party API integrations.

Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account through our platform, we request the following permissions:

  • Login Kit (user.info.basic): We use this to identify which TikTok account is being connected. We retrieve your TikTok user ID and display name to associate your account with our scheduling tools.
  • Content Posting API (video.publish, video.upload): We use these permissions to upload and publish video content to your TikTok profile on your behalf, according to the schedule you set.

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or personal data beyond what is necessary to operate the scheduling service.

How We Use Information

Data accessed through TikTok API is used exclusively to publish video content to the connected user's TikTok account at the times they specify. We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain only the access tokens necessary to maintain your connected account session. You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time, which will revoke our access.

Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with TikTok API in full compliance with TikTok's Terms of Service and Developer Agreement.
