Platform Description

VoxelInk Studios provides a social media content scheduling platform that allows users to connect their TikTok accounts and schedule video posts. Users authorize our platform to publish content to their accounts on their behalf.

TikTok Integration

Our platform integrates with the TikTok API using the following permissions:

  • Login Kit (user.info.basic): Used to authenticate and identify the user's TikTok account when connecting to the platform.
  • Content Posting API (video.publish, video.upload): Used to upload and publish scheduled video content to the user's TikTok profile.

By connecting your TikTok account, you authorize VoxelInk Studios to publish video content to your profile according to the schedule you configure. You may revoke this authorization at any time through your TikTok account settings.
