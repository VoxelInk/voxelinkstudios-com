The Studio

About VoxelInk Studios

VoxelInk Studios creates coloring books for the next generation of gamers and creators. We believe screen time and creativity aren't opposites — and that the best activity book is one kids choose over their tablet.

The Pixel Pals series is our debut collection: three voxel-style coloring books inspired by the block-building worlds kids love. Designed for ages 6–12, equally beloved by nostalgic adults who grew up on pixel games.
