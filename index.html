The Studio

About VoxelInk Studios

VoxelInk Studios is a small studio with a big story - it's built by a mom and her daughter, who dreamed up the very first coloring book at their kitchen table. What started as one idea is now a growing collection, and our youngest artist is still the creative heart of every page.

We make coloring books for the next generation of gamers and creators. We believe screen time and creativity aren't opposites, and that the best activity book is one kids choose over their tablet. From voxel block worlds (ages 6–12) to the kawaii Squishy Critters (ages 5–10), there's a world for every little artist.

Now shipping worldwide on Amazon and Etsy, with more adventures on the way.
